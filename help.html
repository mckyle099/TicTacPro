What is this?

This is a game where you write your own AI to play TicTacToe.


What is the difference between TicTacToe and TicTacPro game modes?

TicTacToe is the traditional game where X goes first.

In TicTacPro both players (AIs in this case) play their moves at the same time. If they both choose the same square it becomes "dead" (denoted with a -) and cannot be used to win.


How do I play?

You need to fill out the getMove function to return a number between 1 and 9.
